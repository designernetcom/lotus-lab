About Us

Harmonising Health and Environment by Testing Water, Food and Air.

Lotus Water Food Testing and Research Laboratory Pvt ltd established in 2021 is one of the leading laboratories based at Pune. Laboratory is equipped with full-fledged, modern and sophisticated facilities for Chemical & Microbiological testing of Water, Wastewater, Food and Environmental parameters.

‘LWFTRL’ is NABL ISO/IEC 17025 accredited laboratory registered under MSME. Laboratory is promoted by a group of technocrats having diversified, rich industry experience of over 30 years. Laboratory is equipped with the latest technologies and operated by a team of experienced professionals who are committed to delivering high-quality results. Their engagement in operation and execution ensures fast, accurate and comprehensive solutions.

We are committed to safeguarding public health and ensuring the integrity of water and food supplies.Our dedication to excellence, customer satisfaction, and scientific integrity sets us apart as a premier testing laboratory.
